Screencasts of all lectures of the Tutorial, and all presentations of the Colloquium, are streamed live using Cisco Webex technology. You can follow the stream directly in your browser. You do not need a Webex account, but you may need to provide an (arbitrary) user name and an (arbitrary) email address.

Please ignore the timings displayed by Webex and refer to the timetables on the Tutorial page and the Colloquium page instead. All times are CEST.
